Description of game:

The object of the game is to match all the cards. When the cards are matched the cards will stay facing up and a sound bit will play that is a reference to the show. If the cards are not matched then the cards will be flipped over and another sound bit will play. While the user is playing the game, there will be a record of the number of games played, attempts per game, and the accuracy per game. The games themes is based off of the fictional character from the show The Office, Dwight K. Schrute. Dwight is a sales man who works at the Dunder Mifflin Paper Company and owns a beet farm. Dwight cousin, Mose, lives with him on his beet farm and is his bestfriend.
